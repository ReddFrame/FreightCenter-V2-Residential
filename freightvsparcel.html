Freight vs. Parcel…

  • Over 150 poounds is freight, under is parcel
  • More than 108 inches in length is freight, under is parcel
  • More than 165 inches in both length and girth combined is freight, below is parcel.
  • Standard services from USPS, UPS and FedEx are used for parcel, not freight. This is where we come in – we can help with both parcel & freight shipping.
